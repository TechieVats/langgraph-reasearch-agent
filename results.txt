Topic: who is the higest scorer in the IPL so far
Quality Score: 8/10

Summary:
The highest scorer in the IPL so far is Virat Kohli, who has amassed 7579 runs in 234 innings, followed closely by Shikhar Dhawan and David Warner. Kohli's tally for the 2016 season is still the record for most runs scored by a player in a single IPL season, and he reclaimed the Orange Cap in 2024. The IPL has seen numerous records broken and created over the years, with players like Chris Gayle, Rohit Sharma, and KL Rahul achieving notable milestones.
